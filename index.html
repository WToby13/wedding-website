Travel Information

›

Getting There

Nearest Airport: Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (NCE)

Direct flights available from major European cities. Transfer time to Mandelieu-La-Napoule: 45 minutes by car or taxi.

Transportation

  • Airport transfers can be arranged
  • Car rental available at the airport
  • Taxi services: approximately €80-100
  • Public transport: Bus 210 from Nice to Cannes, then local bus

Local Transportation

Most activities will be within walking distance or we'll provide shuttle services between venues.

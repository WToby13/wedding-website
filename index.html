Travel Information

›

Getting There

Nearest Airport: Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (NCE)

Mandelieu-La Napoule is approximately 40 minutes from Nice by car.

Transportation Options

  • Rental cars are available directly at the airport
  • Uber and taxis are easily accessible outside arrivals
  • Train: Take the train to Cannes and transfer to the local line for Mandelieu-La Napoule station, which is located in the town center

Local Transportation

Most activities throughout the weekend will be within walking distance.

We will also provide shuttle transportation from the Saturday venue — more details to follow.
